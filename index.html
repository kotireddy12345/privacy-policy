Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to the Campus Cleaning Management App. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • User name
  • Email address
  • User role (Student or Admin)
  • Campus / Block information
  • Complaint details (title, description, location)
  • Uploaded images related to complaints

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow users to submit cleaning complaints
  • To help administrators manage and resolve complaints
  • To track complaint status and updates
  • To improve campus cleanliness and accountability
  • To enhance app functionality and performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using backend services and databases. We use secure APIs and access control to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Image Uploads

Images uploaded with complaints are used only as proof for cleaning issues. These images are visible only to authorized administrators.

5. User Rights

  • You can view your submitted complaints
  • You can request correction of your data
  • You may stop using the app at any time

6. Third-Party Services

This app may use third-party tools such as:

  • Cloud hosting services
  • Image storage services
  • API testing tools (Postman)

7. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for educational institutions. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be updated within the app or website.

9. Contact Us

  • Use the “Contact” option inside the app
